Question Submission Guidelines

To ensure clarity, fairness, and a pleasant experience for all players, please follow the rules below when submitting questions. Our goal is to build a database of accurate, well-written, and enjoyable content.

General Expectations

  • Questions must be clear, concise, and easy to understand.
  • Please pay attention to correct spelling and grammar.
  • Topics should belong to general knowledge or widely known areas.
  • Avoid overly complex, misleading, or ambiguous wording.
  • Answer options should be short and unambiguous.
  • The first answer option should always be the correct one.
  • Incorrect answer options should be relevant to the question's category.

Prohibited Content

  • Offensive, vulgar, or abusive language.
  • Political, religious, or otherwise divisive topics.
  • Personal data or identifiable information.
  • False, misleading, or fabricated claims.

Best Practices

  • Use short sentences and avoid unnecessary filler text.
  • Questions should be accessible and enjoyable for an average player.
  • If your question contains dates, numbers, or facts, double-check their accuracy.

Note: All submitted questions will be reviewed by administrators. Only approved questions will appear in the game.
